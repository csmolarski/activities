Smolarski

UX/UI Design

UX/UI designer with a background in education, research and the media arts, trained at the School of the Art Institute Chicago and the City University of New York. UX/UI Columbia Engineering Bootcamp in 2022. Skilled in the Adobe Creative Suite (Illustrator, Photoshop, Premiere, After Effects and XD), Figma, HTML5, CSS, visual prototyping & wireframing, Design Thinking, qualitative research methodologies (Oral History and Participatory Action Research) and nonfiction storytelling. As an educator and media maker, I have led countless groups of university students to excel in the video art, design and multimedia projects with an emphasis on ideation, teamwork and storytelling. Additional roles include: digital archive project manager, documentary producer/director and co-researcher on projects at the intersection of media and education. My strengths bridge the visual, technical and academic. Could we work together?
